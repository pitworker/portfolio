About Me▮

My name is Sebastian (they/them), and I am a multidisciplinary designer, artist, and maker currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Science and Arts with a focus in Computer Science and Environments Design at Carnegie Mellon University. In my practice, I use code, electronics, and all manner of physical fabrication techniques to create functional, responsive experiences that are both novel and impactful.

My work ranges from UX design to fashion to sculpture, with all sorts of hard-to-categorize artifacts and environments in between. I love having a little bit of every discipline and technique under my belt, and I'm gradually working to become a master generalist.

Feel free to drop me a line, I'm always excited to talk about modern and new media art, leftist politics, queer stuff, linguistics, or whatever you've been making recently :)
